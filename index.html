About Flying Yogurt

Rock isn’t just music—it’s in our blood.

Meet father and son duo Patrick and Ryan Mulhern, and nephew Harrison James — a powerhouse trio spanning three generations of family and friends, fueled by a love for classic rock and a mission to melt faces.

From thunderous guitar solos to pounding drum beats, we bring the energy, the passion, and the soul of rock ‘n’ roll to every stage we hit. With decades of influence running through our veins, we take the music of legends and crank it up to eleven.

Whether we’re jamming the anthems you grew up on or putting our own spin on the classics, one thing is for sure: we rock your socks off—every. single. time. Come see us live. Feel the power. And get ready to rock.
