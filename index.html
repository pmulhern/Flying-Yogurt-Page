About Flying Yogurt

Rock isn't just music—it's in our blood.

Meet father and son duo Patrick and Ryan Mulhern, nephew Harrison James, and longtime family friend Anna O'Brien — a powerhouse band fueled by a love of Classic Rock and a mission to melt faces.

From thunderous guitar solos to pounding drum beats, we bring the energy, passion, and soul of rock 'n' roll to every stage.

Click on our Upcoming Events page, and come see us live! Feel the power. And get ready to rock.

The Flying Yogurt Experience

When we perform at a new venue, something magical happens. The audience starts out warm and supportive—the typical welcome given to any live band. But as our young musicians begin to play, you can see a transformation sweep through the crowd.

Eyes widen. Heads nod with increased appreciation. Casual conversations stop as people turn their full attention to the stage.

It's that moment of recognition: these aren't just young musicians—they're exceptional artists whose skill and passion exceed expectations. By the end of the night, what began as polite applause has transformed into enthusiastic cheers, with audience members often approaching us afterward to express their amazement.

This is the Flying Yogurt Experience! Delivering performances that exceed expectations and create memorable experiences for every audience, whether it's your first time seeing us or your twentieth.
